LIPS Program Checklist Tool
2026 · Digital Healthcare Partners
LIPS Program
Checklist Tool
Internal Web Tool · HTML / CSS / JavaScript
The Problem

At DHP, managing the LIPS government funding program required tracking dozens of required documents across two programs (LIPS I & II) and two types of participants. The process was manual and error-prone — operators had to remember what to collect, from whom, and by when.

What I Built
  • 🗂️
    An interactive checklist tool that guides operators through every required document for LIPS I and LIPS II programs, with progress tracking and filtering by responsible party
  • 📅
    A Timeline Planner — operators enter key dates and the tool auto-generates a customized deadline schedule
  • 🔍
    A Document Checker — upload your files and get instant feedback on whether all required documents are present
  • 💻
    Built entirely in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, runs in any browser, no installation needed
